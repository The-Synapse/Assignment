    Welcome to

    On this website, you are able to play through a series of quizzes to test your general knowledge surrounding history and also more specific history like Inventions. You can choose and start one of these quizzes by hovering on the 'Quiz type selection' drop-down menu and selecting the quiz you would like to try out! Once you complete a quiz you can then view your submitted score(s) on the 'Scoreboard & High-scores' page. Finally, if you would like to learn more about some of the questions that may have come up during one of your quizzes, then you can click on 'Learn about history' for more information.
